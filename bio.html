About

Barbara Mansilla is a paper sculpture artist that received her MFA degree in Illustration from WCSU & her BFA from Paier College of Art. 

 

_______________________________________________________________________________________________________

 

Artist Statement

For me, paper is a fascinating art medium. I like to create whimsical art that captures joy and a sense of wonder. Every new piece I create explores the endless possibilities that paper possesses when it comes to creating rich texture and intricate designs. My goal as an artist is to create artwork that brings out the child within all of us.
